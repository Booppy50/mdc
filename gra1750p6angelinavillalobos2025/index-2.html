Our Story

At BlueWave Seafood Grill, we bring you the freshest catch straight from coastal waters. Every dish is grilled to perfection and served with love. Family-owned since 1998, we’re proud to share the taste of the sea.

Today's Catch

Grilled Mahi-Mahi with Mango Salsa

Experience the perfect blend of savory and tropical flavors with our restaurant-style grilled mahi-mahi. Each fillet is seasoned with olive oil and aromatic spices, then grilled to a tender, flavorful finish.

It’s topped with our signature mango salsa—made with ripe diced mango, red onion, red bell pepper, fresh cilantro, and a splash of lime for brightness. The result is a vibrant, refreshing dish served with a generous spoonful of salsa over the warm, perfectly grilled fish.
